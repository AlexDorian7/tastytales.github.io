Voresonality Test

Find out your Vore Indicator!

Created by TastyTales and Smuxray, with contributions from Eka's Portal users. This test is based off of the Myers-Briggs Personality Indicator, and is designed to show you what kind of predator or prey you are using one of 16 indicators. It is best to answer these questions 'In Character' for the character you want to get a result for. However, you can also answer as yourself!

Please note that this test is meant to be taken for fun, and is still in its development stage. Feedback is appreciated, send your comments to @_TastyTales \n \n CSS by @mikhailchip \n \n Thanks to @Grey_krow for his JavaScript help!
